## PRODUCTION NOTES

- **Lip-sync strategy:** Only Shots 4 and 5 have Mark speaking on camera — shoot/generate 4 from behind-or-side (walking away) and 5 in a loose medium shot so imperfect AI lip-sync doesn't break immersion. Everything else is VO over visuals.
- **UI shots (4, 5, 6, 7):** screen-record the real app. Real UI = proof it exists, not vaporware.
- **Music map:** silence → enters soft at 0:10 → swells at follow-up beat 0:37 → resolves under end card.
- **Hook rule:** the kitchen-doorway blank must land within 2 seconds — it's the retention hook.
- **Voice feature:** shown as working (Shot 4). No "coming soon" caption.

## WHY THIS VERSION IS STRONGER

1. Opens on the single most universal memory failure (walking into a room and blanking) — instant recognition, zero setup.
2. Every feature gets exactly one beat: capture → recall with sources → decision reasoning → proactive follow-up. Nothing repeated, nothing rushed.
3. The interview follow-up replaces the exam — adult, career-stakes, more shareable with your ICP.
4. The last line ("the small stuff was the big stuff") is the caption people will repost.
